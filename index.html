A Little About Me, Siya

Hi, I'm Siya! I'm a new author and a lifelong lover of children's stories. I've been a kindergarten teacher for ten years, and my favorite part of the day has always been storytime.

I was inspired to write 'The Whispering Willow' by the endless curiosity and big hearts of my students. When I'm not writing or in the classroom, I love hiking, drinking tea, and doodling in my sketchbook.

This is my very first book, and I'm so excited to share it with you and your family. I hope it brings a little bit of magic to your storytime, too.
